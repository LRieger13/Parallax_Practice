Hello!

I am a recent graduate and it has been a bit of a quirky journey getting here. It seems like I have always been, what I like to call, Technology field-adjacent. Growing up I pretty much took any old electronic that I cloud get my hands on and take it apart & put it back to together again. I had just learned HTML when the explosion of social media happened, so I would build & rebuild my xanga blogs & myspace pages. Then, when I was a practicing Cosmetologist, I built the little salon I was renting a booth at, a page for scheduling which is what led me to start learning on my own through Codecademy, StackOverflow and the like.

After exhausting my own resources I took the leap and enrolled into Ozark Technical Community College in 2018. After completing a year there we ended up moving back to the Texas area to be closer to family and I transferred to DeVry where I achieved my Bachelors in Computer Information Systems w\ specialization in Web Development & Administration, Summa Cum Laude.
